Alfredo Fettuccine

Recipe for Alfredo Fettuccine Sauce

  • Your choice of meat
  • 1 cup of Milk
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 2 tablespoon of oil
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Butter
  • Green pepper
  • parmesan Cheese
  • Mozeralla Cheese

    • Direction to cook Fettuccine Pasta

    • Add water, fettuccine, salt and 2-3 table spoons of oil in a pot. Boil until cook and drain the water out.

      • Direction to make Alfredo Fettuccine sauce

      • In a pan add oil or butter, your choice of meat and or seafood cook until brown. Add Green pepper, onion chopped, milk and alfredo Sauce stir for 10 minutes. Add Parmesan and Mozeralla cheese and stir until cooked. Layer sauce over pasta.
        • Garnish with dried parsely! Enjoy!
