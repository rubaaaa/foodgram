Chicken Momo

  • Oil
  • Minced Meat
  • 1 onion chopped
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Your choice of vegetables
  • Salt to Taste

    • Momo Dough

    • Oil
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
    • salt to taste

      • Direction

        In a bowl, add flour, salt, baking powder and water. Knead until the dough is soft and stiff.

      Direction to make Momo

      • Heat a tablespoon of oil in a wok and chopped onions, ginger and garlic paste, and minced meat. Cook for 2-3 minutes and add preffered vegetables. Add Pepper and salt to taste. Shape the dough into circular shape and add the fillings in the middle. Seal the momo by pressing the cornors from the bottom to the top egdes. Repeat for the rest. Momo can be fried on pan or boiled using a dimsum steamer.
        • Serve Chicken Momos with any sauce you prefer! Enjoy!
