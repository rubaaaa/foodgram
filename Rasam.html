Rasam - South Indian Pepper Rasam

Preparation for Rasam

  • Oil
  • 2-3 Tomatoes
  • Tamrind Water
  • Peppercorn
  • Pepper Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Curry Leaves
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Red Chillies Dry
  • 4-5 Garlic Cloves Minced
  • Corriander
  • Salt

    • Direction to Rasam

    • Add 4-5 spoons of oil, add Cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let it fry for 20-30 seconds.
    • Add curry leaves, Dry red chillies, minced garlic, 1-1/2 spoon of pepper powder and 1-1/2 spoon of cumin powder.
    • Add sliced Tomaotes and let fry for bit, ensure to smash it until it comes chunky and into pieces .
    • Add Tamrind water and garnish with Corriander leaves.
    • Add Salt to taste
      • Enjoy!
