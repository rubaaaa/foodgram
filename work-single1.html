Nachos

Snack

Nachos are a Mexican dish which consists of heated tortilla chips with topings of choice and topped with melted cheese. Often served as a snack or appetizer.

Recipe

  • 1 large bag of tortilla chips
  • Vegetable oil
  • Ground beef/ Chicken
  • Green Pepper
  • Green onions
  • Any toppings of your choice

    • Directions

  • Preheat Oven to 350F
  • Garnish all toppings of your favourite choice
  • Garnish any cheese topings
  • Toast the cheaps for 5-10 minutes in the oven
    • Enjoy!
